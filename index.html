What About "Duplicate Content" And Search Engine Ranking?

Duplicate content was a concern years ago when spammers would create 150 pages on their “websites” and each one of these 150 pages would be exactly the same, down to the letter ( Basically they were trying to Overload Specific Keywords in order to rank higher in search results). Obviously, this created weak websites that contained no value whatsoever to the user! Search engines consider “duplicate content” to be websites that have the same content over and over again, on hundreds of pages, for the specific reason of trying to “spam” traffic. Unless you are a robot or trying to “spam” search results, you don't have to worry about this at all. If you are a real person, with a real business, and your website has some of the same/ similar content as another site, very rarely and most likely never will it impact your search ratings!

Our team has a number of procedures for ensuring that this is not a problem for our customers! We cap the total number of Guidebooks sold and we continuously re-write the content to allow for significant variations to keep the content fresh from a SEO standpoint.
